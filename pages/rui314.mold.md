- mold: A Modern Linker

	- ![image.png](../assets/image_1643356665655_0.png)

- 实际体验的感觉确实还不错，缺点是不支持 [[Windows]] 和 [[MacOS]]
-
- Rust 配置
	- ```toml
	  [target.x86_64-unknown-linux-gnu]
	  linker = "clang"
	  rustflags = ["-C", "link-arg=-fuse-ld=/path/to/mold"]
	  ```
